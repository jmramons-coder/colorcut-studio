Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

ColorCut is designed as a quiet kids activity. The current web app does not require an account, does not show ads, does not include chat or uploads, and does not intentionally collect personal information from children.

Information we collect

The current app stores no child profile, name, email address, photo, message, or drawing upload. Puzzle progress is kept only in the running browser session.

Payments and parent links

Paid features are not connected yet. When payments are added, purchase links will be placed behind a parent gate and this policy will be updated before launch.

Cookies and analytics

The current app does not use analytics scripts, advertising cookies, or third-party ad networks. Vercel may process basic server logs to deliver and secure the site.

Children's privacy

ColorCut is intended to avoid collecting personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a future feature, contact us so it can be removed.

Contact

Parent contact email: hello@colorcut.studio. This policy will be updated before any paid feature launches.
